About this plan

Παιδεία is a 156-week formation in Greek across three years — Modern Greek to conversational fluency in Year 1, Koine New Testament and Apostolic Fathers in Year 2, Patristic depth (Chrysostom capstone) in Year 3.

At 30–45 minutes weekdays and 60+ minutes weekends, you'll arrive at functional fluency in both Modern and Patristic Greek — able to read the Fathers with a lexicon, follow the Liturgy with comprehension, and converse in Greece.

If you fall off

You will miss days. You will miss weeks. This is not failure — it is being human across 1,092 days.

Missed 1–3 days? Just resume at today. Don't make up missed work. Your streak resets but your progress is intact.

Missed a week? Don't restart the week. Resume at the current calendar week. Spend Saturday reviewing what you missed.

Missed a month or more? Go back two weeks from where you stopped and resume there. Re-listen, re-read. Don't restart from week 1 — you haven't lost what you learned.

The work you did is still in you. Greek doesn't disappear. Just begin again, today.
